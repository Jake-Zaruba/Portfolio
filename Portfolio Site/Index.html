ABOUT

Hey, I'm Jake.

I've been running a wedding photography business full time since 2015.
I love creating, and after 130 weddings, I felt as though I was no longer growing or challenging myself. When I first started my business, I made my website using a wordpress template. I was obsessed with customizing as many features as I could, but with my limited knowledge at the time, I had to compromise.


Six years later in 2021, I decided to dive into web development with the goal of re-creating my website the way I had intended all those years ago. That project grew into an absolute obsession with web design and development. Shortly after that project, I applied for an IT support role with J.J. Keller to take a step towards a career in tech, and I'm now ready to take my next step.
